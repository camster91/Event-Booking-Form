Event Hall Includes:

✅ HDMI and VGA cable for laptop connection.
✅ Background Music requires Lectern PC to be in the room.
✅ Presentation clicker with laser.
✅ Confidence monitor.
✅ Either Clear Acrylic Podium or Lectern(PC) with goose-neck microphone.
✅ Up to 6 lapel microphones.
✅ Up to 30 handheld microphones for panels and Q&A.
✅ Image displayed on the Media wall starting one hour before the event start time*.

Fleck Atrium Includes:

✅ 1 Technician (available one hour before the event start time).
✅ Acrylic Podium with a mic.
✅ HDMI for laptop connection.
✅ Presentation clicker with laser.
✅ Up to 10 mics (any combination of either lapel or handheld microphones which totals 10 microphones).

*Media-wall images: to be submitted one week in advance in JPEG or PNG format (wide-screen). The Media-wall does not technically show PowerPoint. However, you can use PowerPoint to design the slides, then “save as” an image. The wall can accommodate multiple images in a loop. If you have more than 5, please save as a video. If you’re using a PowerPoint loop with animations, use the “save as” function in PowerPoint to convert the loop to a video file.

** IMAG: "image magnification" used in the audiovisual production industry. It refers to large-scale theatrical or concert video projection to enable audience members seated at great distances from the stage to see details of the performer's body language and facial expressions that could not be seen with the unaided eye.
